How to use

  1. Copy HTML from your editor into "Source HTML".
  2. Prepare List: Keyword, URL.
  3. Paste List into "Keywords & URLs".
  4. Select Rules:
    • Exclude Headers or Divs.
    • Or enable "Only Paragraphs" to be strict.
  5. Click Weave Links.

How to use

  1. Copy the HTML from your blog post editor (WordPress, Ghost, etc.) and paste it into the "Source Code" box.
  2. Prepare your links: Create a list where each line is: Keyword, URL.
  3. Paste the list into the "Keywords & URLs" box.
  4. Select Options: Choose your link type (nofollow, etc.) and open behavior.
  5. Click Weave Links.
  6. Copy the Resulting HTML and paste it back into your blog editor.
